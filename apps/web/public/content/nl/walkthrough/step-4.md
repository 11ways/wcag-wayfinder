# Tags & Favorites

This pane shows your active tags (up to 3) and match counts.

**Click any tag** in the criteria details to filter by it. Remove tags individually or clear all at once.

**Other features:**
- **⭐ Favorites** - Star criteria to save them for later
- **🔗 Shareable URLs** - Bookmark or share your exact filtered view

You're all set! Happy exploring! 🎉
